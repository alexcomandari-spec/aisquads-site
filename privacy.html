2. Information we collect

We collect the following categories of information to operate the Service:

  • Client account information. When a business engages AI Squads, we collect contact details such as company name, contact name, email address, phone number, and billing information.
  • WhatsApp conversation data. The Service processes messages sent to and from our clients' WhatsApp Business numbers, including message text, voice notes, images, and other media exchanged between our clients and their end users.
  • End-user contact identifiers. To deliver and route conversations, we process end users' WhatsApp identifiers (typically a phone number), display names, and conversation metadata such as timestamps.
  • Service configuration data. Prompts, scripts, and configuration files used to operate each client's AI agent.
  • Technical and log data. System logs, error reports, and usage metrics needed to operate, secure, and improve the Service.

3. How we use information

We use the information described above to:

  • Provide, operate, and maintain the AI WhatsApp automation Service;
  • Generate, deliver, and log automated replies to end-user messages;
  • Transcribe voice notes and generate voice responses where the Service is configured to do so;
  • Communicate with our clients about their account, billing, and the Service;
  • Diagnose problems, prevent abuse, and improve the reliability and quality of the Service; and
  • Comply with our legal obligations and enforce our Terms of Service.
